About Us

Saankethika 2K18

The objective of the symposium is to bring the young budding engineering graduates together to present their findings and discuss future trends, directions and new challenges in the Electronics & Communication field. This symposium aims to intensify the information exchange of their ideas in theoretical research and practical developments.


This symposium can be used to discuss what and how the student community can facilitate the process of technology development. This would prove to be a boon to students. It will provide a platform to discuss the strengths and weakness involved in sustainability of innovation in the Electronics & Communication field. It will be a forum for new ideas and concepts and also would endeavour to examine the different dimensions of Electronics & Communication field.
